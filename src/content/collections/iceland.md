Iceland never lets you forget where you are. Every turn reveals a landscape that feels impossible — waterfalls taller than buildings, beaches made of volcanic glass, glaciers that creak and groan in the silence. Two weeks driving the Ring Road, sleeping in the car, chasing the midnight sun.
